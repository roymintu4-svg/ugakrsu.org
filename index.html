About Us

Ugakrsu represents the collective voice of the Koch Rajbongshi students from the historic Undivided Goalpara region. We trace our lineage back to the glorious Kamata-Koch Kingdom, upholding the spirit of Bir Chilarai in our pursuit of social justice, constitutional ST status, and educational excellence.
